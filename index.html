Moon Landings

  1. Apollo 11

July 20, 1969

Apollo 11 was an American spaceflight in which the astronauts Neil Armstrong
and Buzz Aldrin became the first ever to land on the Moon on July 20, 1969, at
20:18 UTC. Further, Neil Armstrong became the first to step onto the Moon 6
hours after the landing on 21 July, 02:56 UTC.
